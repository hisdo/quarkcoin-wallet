Have your Dimecoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Dimecoin Wallet is the first mobile Dimecoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Dimecoin amount in QRK and mQRK.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Dimecoin via NFC, QR-codes or Dimecoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Dimecoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Dimecoin balance.

You can find more about Dimecoin at
http://www.dimecoin.co/

If you want to contribute to Dimecoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/HashEngineering/Dimecoin-wallet
